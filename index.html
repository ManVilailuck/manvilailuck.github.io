My name is Chaichanok Vilailuck, but I go by Man. I am currently a part-time student at the University of California, Berkeley working towards my Master's degree in Information and Data Science. I work on machine learning systems that can operate reliably within the real constraints of infrastructure, public services, and institutional workflows. My research interests include robust retrieval-augmented ML, stochastic modeling for large-scale systems, and the governance and safety of AI in high-stakes settings. Long-term, I aim to bridge rigorous ML research with the practical realities of national-scale technology deployment.
